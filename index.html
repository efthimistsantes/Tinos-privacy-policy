# Privacy Policy **Last updated:** March 2026 ## 1. Introduction This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information when you use our mobile application (“the App”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how data is used. --- ## 2. Information We Collect ### a. Device Information We may collect certain device-related information, including: * Device identifiers (such as advertising ID – IDFA) * Basic usage data (e.g., app interactions) This information is primarily collected through third-party services such as advertising providers. --- ### b. Location Data With your permission, the App may access your device’s precise location in order to: * Provide weather information * Suggest nearby places and experiences Location data is used only within the app and is **not linked to your identity**. --- ### c. Advertising Data We use third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob) which may collect: * Device identifiers * Advertising interaction data This data may be used for: * Displaying relevant ads * Measuring ad performance --- ## 3. How We Use Your Information We use collected data for the following purposes: * **App Functionality**: To provide core features such as weather and location-based suggestions * **Analytics**: To understand how users interact with the app and improve features * **Advertising**: To display ads via third-party services --- ## 4. Tracking & Advertising The App may request permission to track your activity across apps and websites using Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework. If you allow tracking: * We and our advertising partners may use your device identifier to show more relevant ads If you deny tracking: * Ads may still be shown, but they will be less personalized --- ## 5. Data Sharing We do **not** sell your personal data. We may share limited data with trusted third-party partners, including: * Advertising providers (e.g., Google AdMob) These partners process data according to their own privacy policies. --- ## 6. Data Linking We do **not** link collected data to your identity. We do not collect: * Names * Emails * Accounts or user profiles --- ## 7. Your Choices You can: * Disable location access in your device settings * Deny tracking via the App Tracking Transparency prompt * Reset your advertising identifier in device settings --- ## 8. Children’s Privacy The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. --- ## 9. Security We take reasonable measures to protect your information.
